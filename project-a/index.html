What we know so far about NeoGenesis? “It is a newly discovered species living in the waters of Antarctica, thriving in extremely low temperatures. Due to its life in the dark depths of the ocean, its color is deep blue. So far, only one individual has been found, and it is not easy to survive. It enjoys wandering among aquatic plants, with ice attached to its surroundings.Its diet consists of marine pollutants such as plastic and discarded metals, though it consumes only small amounts at a time. After eating, it grows larger, but excessive consumption will lead to its death. Once it reaches a certain size, it will reproduce on its own. However, its offspring remain attached to the mother and cannot survive independently. If the mother dies, the offspring will also perish.Currently, it is being preserved in the ZACH Laboratory.”
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Click the mouse to feed it. And click the keyboard to make it move slowly.
